Accessibility - BeatWave Music Player

Accessibility Commitment

1. Our Goal

We are committed to making BeatWave Music Player accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities.

2. Features for Accessibility

- Keyboard navigation support.
- Screen reader compatibility.
- High contrast mode options.
- Adjustable font sizes for readability.

3. User Feedback

We encourage feedback from our users to help improve our accessibility features. Reach out to us with any concerns or suggestions.

4. Future Improvements

We continuously work on improving accessibility by following global standards and best practices.
