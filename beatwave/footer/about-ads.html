About Ads

1. Why Do We Show Ads?

Advertisements help us keep BeatWave Music Player free for users. They support our platform while allowing us to improve and provide new features.

2. Personalized Ad Experience

We use cookies and user preferences to display ads that are relevant to your interests, ensuring a better experience.

3. Types of Ads We Display

Audio Ads: Played between songs on the free version.
Banner Ads: Displayed in non-intrusive areas of the player.
Video Ads: Occasionally shown before accessing certain premium content.

4. Managing Ad Preferences

Users can manage their ad preferences through their account settings. Premium users can opt for an ad-free experience.

5. Contact Us

If you have concerns about our ad policies or want more information, reach out to us through our support page.
