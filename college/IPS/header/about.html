About Organization

INDIRA PRIYADARSHINI COLLEGE, CHHINDWARA was Founded in 2001, this constituent College of Chhindwara University Chhindwara [Raja Shankar Shah University] and Recognizes By the Higher Education Department Gov. Of M.P. College is Listed by the University Grant Commision (UGC) Under section 2(f).


Within a short span of time, our college o\is emerging as a benchmark for continuous journey towards excellence and has achieved remarkable recognition as one of the prestigious and progressive educational institution in Madhya Pradesh.


Our College offers Bachelor of Science courses in Interdisplenary Sciences, Microbiology, Biotechnology, Mathematics, Plain Biology, Computer Sciences & Information Technology, Management, Commerce. All Academic Programs Offered by the college has strong support infrastructure in competent and dedicated faculty, requisite classrooms, state-of-the art laboratories, sp, extensive library facility and intellectual environment. All the efforts are undertaken to provide a platform for nurturing the talent of young minds by-stimulating them to pursue excellence.


Our students have invariably excelled in the university exams with positions of merit. Activities that fall outside the realm of the usual curriculum plays a vital role in imbibing strong ethical values in the students and to transform them into ignited individuals and competitive professionals so that they can reach to the pinnacle of success in their field. With perspective in mind. There is a continuous endeavour to ensure that the students evolve into near perfect entities through a synergy of academic education and various vibrant extracurricular and co-curricular activities under the aegis of various educational clubs and societies.


The college also offers opportunities to learn several contemporary subjects in the form of short term add on courses being taught by experts from within and outside. The college tries to infuse self confidence in our students and help them develop the skills and demeanour of lifelong learning so that they become academically competent and capable of facing the future with resilience and optimism.
